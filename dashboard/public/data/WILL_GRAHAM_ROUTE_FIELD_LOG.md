## How To Use

Just talk like a driver. No format required. Example:

  "Ran Hartford to Providence today. I-95 North was clean at 09:00.
   No issues. GPS route was fine, I followed it."

  "Ran into Baltimore off I-95. Exit 47 approach to the DC is brutal —
   tight streets, took 40 minutes to go 3 miles. Avoid if possible."

  "Rest area on I-81 South near Carlisle PA is solid. Big lot,
   easy in and out with a 70ft rig. Good overnight option."

Include:
- origin and destination or general area
- route or highway used
- what was good or bad
- time of day or day of week if relevant
- any stop observations (parking, approach, suitability)
